Secrets

  • Bananas are berries but strawberries are not, botanically speaking.
  • Sharks existed before trees – they’ve been around for over 400 million years.
  • Octopuses have three hearts, and two of them stop beating when they swim.
  • Honey never spoils – archaeologists found 3,000-year-old honey in Egyptian tombs that’s still edible.
  • Your stomach gets a new lining every 3–4 days so it doesn’t digest itself.
  • Sloths can hold their breath longer than dolphins – up to 40 minutes by slowing their heart rate.
